Frequently Asked Questions

Browse our comprehensive FAQ section to find quick answers regarding our loan products, application process, documentation, and more.

What documents are required for a personal loan?

Typically, we require identity proof (e.g., Aadhar, PAN), address proof (e.g., utility bill), and income proof (e.g., salary slips, bank statements, ITR). Additional documents may be requested based on your profile and loan type.

How long does the loan approval process take?

Once all required documents are submitted and verified, personal loans can be approved within 24-48 hours. Home and business loans might take slightly longer, usually 3-7 business days, depending on the complexity of verification.

Can I apply for a loan if I have a low credit score?

While a healthy credit score is beneficial, we evaluate each application comprehensively. We may offer specific loan products or alternative solutions for applicants with lower credit scores, potentially with different terms or collateral requirements.

What are the repayment options available?

We offer flexible repayment options including EMI via auto-debit from your bank account, online payments through our portal, and cheque/cash payments at our designated branches or partner locations. We aim to make repayment convenient for you.

Is there any pre-payment penalty?

Our policy on pre-payment penalties varies by specific loan product and terms. Some loans may offer partial or full pre-payment without penalty, while others might have a nominal charge. Please refer to your specific loan agreement or consult with our advisors for details.
